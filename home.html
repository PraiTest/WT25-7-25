About CVR

CVR College of Engineering, Hyderabad, is a premier institution committed to innovation, research, and quality education, affiliated with JNTU Hyderabad and approved by AICTE.

Established in 2001, it offers a vibrant environment with state-of-the-art infrastructure and a focus on holistic student development across various engineering disciplines.

Join CVR to be part of a dynamic, inspiring community shaping tomorrow's leaders and innovators through excellence in academics and research.
